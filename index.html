About Varanasi

Shree Kashi Vishwanath Temple

One of the most sacred temples in India, dedicated to Lord Shiva, located in the heart of Varanasi.

Ganga Aarti

A divine ritual performed at the ghats of Varanasi to honor the holy river Ganges.

Dashashwamedh Ghat

One of the most prominent and bustling ghats in Varanasi, known for its stunning aarti ceremonies.

Sarnath

A significant Buddhist site, where Lord Buddha gave his first sermon after attaining enlightenment.

Namo Ghat

A newly developed ghat, known for its peaceful environment and modern amenities.

Bharat Kala Bhavan

Bharat Kala Bhavan in Varanasi is a premier museum showcasing India's rich cultural and artistic heritage through its vast collection of artifacts and artworks.

Ramnagar Fort

Ramnagar Fort in Varanasi is a historic fort on the banks of the Ganges, known for its stunning architecture and royal heritage.

Bhu Vishwanath Temple

Bhu Vishwanath Temple in Varanasi is a revered Hindu shrine dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its spiritual significance and intricate architecture.
