About Kashika Travel

Kashika Travel is a trusted travel operator based in Varanasi, serving pilgrims and travelers across the region. We operate a modern fleet — from compact sedans to tempo travellers — and maintain high standards of safety, cleanliness, and hospitality. Our drivers are professional, experienced, and ready to assist with local knowledge to make your journey comfortable.

Our Fleet & Services

We provide well-maintained vehicles including Tata Etios, Dzire, Toyota Innova and Tempo Traveller (9+1). 24/7 support and personalized itineraries are available on request.
